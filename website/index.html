Herm0ni

AI Chess Bot

What is Herm0ni

Herm0ni is an artificial intelligence chess bot that is playable on lichess.org. Using bitboards, alpha beta search and a custom heuristic algorithm, Herm0ni is capabale of playing games of chess against both humans and other chess bots. The purpose of Herm0ni is to offer another bot for people to play against, whether that be for practice or for fun. This project was also used for my personal development as a programmer.

Technologies Used

  • lichess-bot api
